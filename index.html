About us

Problem

The common problem is that it’s very difficult to get an appointment with a good physio because of the queue. Yet you’re in pain and need help as soon as possible. Worse, sometimes you have to wait and endure the pain until you are seen!

Solution

Luckily, we have done all the hard work for you. We have carefully selected a list of the top Physiotherapists. Now you can contact them by submitting a general quick response request. The one who is most available and responsive will reply!

Worthwhile

It is a win-win for both sides! You benefit because you can quickly find a suitable physiotherapist who is currently available to see you. The physiotherapists benefit because they can fill their schedules more quickly. Everybody wins!

Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

How much do the services of a physiotherapist cost?

The exact price depends on the complexity of the specific case and the qualifications of the physiotherapist. The total cost of the service will also depend on the number and frequency of sessions. The physiotherapist will give you more precise information about the price of the services you need when he or she responds to your request.

Which locations do you work in?

We refer to clinics and practices in different locations. Some physiotherapists provide home services at the specified address. Some physiotherapists provide services in the districts of the specified cities. All you need to do is fill in the request form and specify the address or a convenient neighbourhood. The fastest, but also the closest physiotherapist will respond to the request.

Are all of the physiotherapists qualified?

Without a doubt, we have selected only the best specialists. These are carefully selected professionals with the appropriate qualifications, educated in the best healthcare and medical institutions. Before being included in our list of selected specialists, they had to provide feedback from existing clients and undergo an interview with the “Kizi M” quality control team. The more complex your case requires treatment, the higher qualified physiotherapy specialist will assist you. If you have any additional questions regarding the qualifications of the physiotherapist, feel free to ask the specialist who responded to your inquiry.
